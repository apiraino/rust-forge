# Social media

The Rust project maintains an official presence on different social media platforms. The following accounts are curated by the [social media team][soc-med-team], credentials are maintained by the [infrastructure team][infra-team].

[soc-med-team]: https://github.com/rust-lang/team/blob/HEAD/teams/social-media.toml
[infra-team]: https://github.com/rust-lang/team/blob/HEAD/teams/infra.toml

## Fediverse

* [`@rust`](https://social.rust-lang.org/@rust)

## Bluesky

* [`rust-lang.org`](https://bsky.app/profile/rust-lang.org)

## Twitter

The Rust project used to have an official presence on Twitter. While the following accounts are still active and
preserved to avoid profile squatting, they are not actively used.

* [`@rustlang`](https://twitter.com/rustlang)
* [`@RustStatus`](https://twitter.com/RustStatus)
* [`@cratesiostatus`](https://twitter.com/cratesiostatus)
* [`@rustasync`](https://twitter.com/rustasync)
